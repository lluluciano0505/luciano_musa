*Case study written for MUSA 5080: Public Policy Analytics, University of Pennsylvania.*

This case study examines the 2013 closure of Sisters Nightclub — Philadelphia's last lesbian bar — and its replacement by Franky Bradley's, tracing how gentrification, economic precarity, and shifting social norms intersect in the nighttime economy. It concludes with policy recommendations for Philadelphia's Nighttime Economy Strategic Plan.
