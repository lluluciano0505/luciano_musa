This project maps fire alarm patterns across Philadelphia against building characteristics, neighborhood demographics, and housing age to identify predictable risk clusters.

{% include image.html url="https://lluluciano0505.github.io/Jingqi_Data_Policy_Portfolio/assignments/Final/FINAL_1.html" image="projects/proj-2/thumbnail.png" %}

The output is a policy recommendation for a targeted inspection and retrofit program, with resource allocation guided by the model's predictions and tested for racial equity.
