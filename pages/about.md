# Jingqi Lu

I'm Jingqi, a graduate student in [Urban Spatial Analytics (MUSA)](https://www.design.upenn.edu/urban-spatial-analytics) at the University of Pennsylvania. My work focuses on spatial data science, mobility, urban equity, and AI tools for making messy urban information more usable.

I studied Geography with Social Data Science at [University College London](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/), spent time at the University of Amsterdam, and now live in Philadelphia. Across those places, I've become interested in how cities distribute access, movement, and opportunity, and how spatial analysis can help make those patterns visible.

I work with Python, R, SQL, ArcGIS, and geospatial machine learning, and I'm currently collaborating with [Henning Larsen](https://henninglarsen.com/) on data auditing and urban dataset evaluation for design practice.

Outside work, I play tennis, guitar, and keep up my Dutch. You can reach me at [lluluciano0505@gmail.com](mailto:lluluciano0505@gmail.com), or find me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jingqi-lu-04a910266) and [GitHub](https://github.com/lluluciano0505).
